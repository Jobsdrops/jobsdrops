Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026/02/11

1. Introduction

JobsDrops respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

JobsDrops does not require users to create accounts and does not collect personal information for browsing or accessing listings.

The only personal information we may receive is:

  • Email address and message content when a user voluntarily contacts us.

3. How We Use Information

Any email communication received is used solely for responding to inquiries, correcting information, or handling partnership or support requests. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

4. Browser-Based Functionality

Certain tools on this website operate entirely within the user’s browser environment. Information entered into forms such as the CV builder is processed locally and is not stored on our servers.

5. Cookies and Third-Party Services

JobsDrops may use third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers. These services may use cookies or similar technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users may disable cookies through their browser settings.

6. External Links

JobsDrops provides links to external websites for official applications and information. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure that any personal information voluntarily provided through email is handled securely and only retained for as long as necessary to respond to the inquiry.

8. Your Rights

In accordance with POPIA, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided to us.

Requests can be submitted via our Contact page.

9. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or operational practices.
